# Purpose

The Color Layer defines the perceived color of hosiery after interacting with transparency, material, lighting, and skin tone.

Unlike ordinary garments, hosiery color is never independent.

Final perceived color is determined by:

Color × Opacity × Material × Lighting × Skin Tone

Therefore, color should always be described as a visual appearance rather than a simple color name.

---

# AI Interpretation Priority

When rendering hosiery colors, AI models generally interpret:

1. Base Color
2. Transparency
3. Skin Influence
4. Surface Reflection
5. Lighting

# Color Family System

---

# 1. Black Family

The most common commercial hosiery color.

---

## Ultra Sheer Black

Recommended for:

VOL-1 ~ VOL-2

Core Keywords

```text id="bk01"
soft black tint
transparent black veil
smoky black
barely-there black
```

Visual Characteristics

* skin remains dominant
* soft smoky appearance
* no solid black areas
* elegant transparency

---

## Semi Sheer Black

Recommended for:

VOL-3 ~ VOL-4

Core Keywords

```text id="bk02"
semi-transparent black
balanced black tone
natural black hosiery
soft charcoal black
```

Visual Characteristics

* natural leg enhancement
* moderate skin visibility
* commercial standard appearance

---

## Opaque Black

Recommended for:

VOL-5 ~ VOL-8

Core Keywords

```text id="bk03"

```

Visual Characteristics

* uniform black
* minimal skin visibility
* premium appearance

# Color × Opacity Rules

Ultra Sheer

Color should softly tint the skin.

Never appear solid.

---

Semi Sheer

Color should balance skin visibility and fabric tone.

---

Opaque

Color should be uniform.

Skin should not dominate.

---

# Color × Finish Rules

Matte

Colors become softer.

---

Satin

Colors become richer.

---

Gloss

Colors become brighter.

---

Velvet

Colors become deeper.

# Common Mistakes

❌ Using only:

```text id="cm01"
black tights
```

Prefer:

```text id="cm02"
semi-transparent charcoal black hosiery
```

---

❌ Using white hosiery with strong gloss

Produces unrealistic plastic appearance.

---

❌ Using nude without matching skin tone

Causes unnatural color mismatch.
